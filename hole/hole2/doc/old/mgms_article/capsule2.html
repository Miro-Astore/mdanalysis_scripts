Pore Dimensions of Ion Channels: A capsule fitting inside the pore of gramicidin


A capsule fitting inside the pore of gramicidin

The capsule is shown as a red grid surface fitting inside the van der Waals surface of the pore lining residues, shown as green dots. The axis of the capsule is shown as a light blue line. The view is almost exactly down the channel vector.
